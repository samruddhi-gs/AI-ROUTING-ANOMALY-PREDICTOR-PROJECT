About AI Guard Pro Engine

Neural Anomaly Predictor (NAP) v2.0

AI Guard Pro utilizes advanced Graph Neural Networks (GNN) to model the global internet routing topology and detect anomalies in BGP updates. The system analyzes both spatial and temporal patterns in routing behavior to predict suspicious network activities before they spread.

Model Architecture

Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) combined with attention mechanisms for analyzing sequential BGP routing data.

Data Sources

Real-time ingestion from global routing collectors including RIPE RIS and RouteViews.
